About Us

We Are Here to manage Food waste and garbage

"Annapurna" is an organizations that works towards reducing food waste and providing food to people in need. It is a non-profit network of food banks that provides food and groceries to need ful people. The organization sources food from Hotels, Restaurants and Functions and redirects it to people in need, helping to reduce waste while also feeding those who are struggling to put food on the table.
